About
I work on multimodal and temporal deep learning, spanning clinical time-series modeling (EHR), medical imaging and computational pathology, and video understanding. My recent work includes learning robust representations over time, generative modeling for computational staining/destaining, and video-based action recognition and captioning.

Research
My research focuses on learning reliable, interpretable representations from multimodal data, with an emphasis on temporal modeling. I develop methods for clinical decision support using EHR trajectories, generative modeling for computational pathology, and video understanding for action recognition and captioning. Across these domains, I am interested in robust generalization, evaluation over time, and practical deployment in real-world pipelines.
